    01The Host

    A musician who kept the mic on.

    I'm Chazdyn — a musician and late-night host from Washington State. What began as game streams has become something I care about far more: live music, long-form conversation, and the occasional guest who turns an ordinary night into one worth remembering.

    Some nights it's an acoustic set. Others it's a sit-down that runs long because the story's too good to cut short. Either way, the room stays warm and the mic stays on.
