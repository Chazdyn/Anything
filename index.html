01The Host

A musician who left the room rolling.

Music has been the constant my whole life — long before any of it was on camera. The stream started as a place to share the games I love, then slowly turned into a studio, workshop, book club, comedy detour, and whatever else the room can survive.

Now I'm building toward the things I actually want to make: live sets straight from the room, a morning show with late-night DNA, creator tools that make streams feel custom-built, and recurring bits that are strange enough to earn their own lore.
